About the Hood

Under The Hood is an experiment bringing together leading developers and designers with national experts in social finance, education, crowdfunding, sharing platforms, and other leaders of the new economy to envision, prototype, and deploy innovate solutions that reimagine our systems of exchange from the ground up.

Focusing on Singapore's Bukit Ho Swee neighbourhood, the initiative will answer pressing questions, such as:

  • How can financial data empower low-income residients?
  • How can local currencies support local businesses and community organizations
  • How can sharing platforms be tailored to fit the needs of under-served communities?
  • How can tools like crowdfunding and microcredit be put to work for social service organizations and individuals alike?
